Who is Meezan Jafri

Meezaan Jafri was born in 1989 (28 years; age in 2018) in Mumbai. He went to Ecole Mondiale World School, Mumbai. After completing his schooling, he did his graduation in business. It was after he graduated in business that he went to School of Visual Arts, New York to pursue a course in filmmaking. Jafri began his career as an Assistant Director for Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s film “Bajirao Mastani". When he was in school, he was more inclined towards music and sports. He has represented the state of Maharashtra in many inter-and state-level Basketball competitions. Moreover, Meezan also played football and did swimming.

Mizaan is the eldest child of Javed Jaffrey. He was born into a family of actors. His grandfather, popularly known as Jagdeep, is a veteran actor and comedian; his paternal uncle Naved Jaffery is an actor, producer, director, and choreographer.
