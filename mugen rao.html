Who is Mugen Rao

Mugan Rao, also known as MGR, was born on 20 October 1995 (22 years old; age in 2018) in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. His father was a stage performer and, so, he started performing along with his father to overcome financial constraints. Becoming an actor was a childhood dream for Mugen. He made his debut with the movie “Senandung Malam.” Apart from being an actor, Mugen Rao is also a singer, lyricist, and an entrepreneur.Mugan Rao was born into a middle-class Hindu Family to Prakash Rao Krishnan, a stage performer, and Nirmala Devi, a worker in SP Setia Infrastructure company. He has a brother, Vignesh Rao, and a sister, Janany.

Mugan Rao completed his schooling from a school in Kuala Lumpur. He obtained a Diploma in Performing Arts in 2015 from Limkokwing University.Because of the financial obstacles faced by his family, Mugen Rao started his career when he was 9 years old with performing on the stage with his father. While he was still studying in school, he formed a songwriting-producing-singing group called “Random.CROWNZ” along with his classmates.When he was 13 years old, Mugen’s uncle Sathis Rao helped him get a role in a Malay Film. Therefore, Mugen began his professional journey with a small role of a paperboy in the film “Senandung Malam.” Though the role was small, Mugen has not looked back since then. His portrayal in “Senandung Malam” led him another role in “Gerak Khas.”
