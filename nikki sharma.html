Who is Nikki Sharma

Nikki Sharma was born on 23 January 1996 (23 years old; age in 2019) in Lajpat Nagar, New Delhi. Nikki started her acting career in 2015 with Disney’s mini-series “Kabhi Aise Geet Gaya Karo.” In 2016, she acted in Star Plus’ “Dehleez” and Colors TV’s “Krishnadasi.” In the same year, Nikki Sharma entered Colors TV’s “Sasural Simar Ka.” Sharma also played a role in Colors TV’s “Roop-Mard Ka Naya Swaroop.” In 2019, Nikki Sharma appeared in Amazon Prime’s web-series “Mind the Malhotras” in which Mini Mathur and Cyrus Sahukar played the lead roles.She belongs to a Brahmin Family. Her mother’s name is Sandeepa Sharma.
